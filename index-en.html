Project Introduction

Design an loading and unloading device for the robot production line

The main task of this project is to design an automatic lifting table device to assist workers in loading and unloading assembled robots on the robot production line of the company Ecovacs.

The total duration of the project is 30 days from June 27, 2022 to July 27, 2022 (delivery date).

This project will be completed by a group of 3 people with the following job assignments.

  • Project Manager(PM)Chengzhangzheng Wu
  • Electronic Engineer(EE)Taimingwang Liu
  • Manufacturing Engineer(ME)Hanyang Xu

Process of structural design

At first, we were looking for innovation and wanted to adopt an elevator structure for lifting. However, we did not consider the actual eas of operation for workers.

So we set out to design the next version...

Mr.Wang from Ecovacs

What we need is a rising platform, not an elevator car

After that, we are going to use two motors to lift simultaneously to achieve the target effect.

However, we do not take into account the actual working process, the two motors with their own losses and other factors, they are difficult to ensure synchronous rotation, and may even cause motor derailment.

So we set out to design the next version...

In the third version of the design, our ME proposed to use a combination of gears and racks, based on the second version, to drive the whole structure by a separate motor. Only the gears and racks did not fit well and the motor was attached to the plate causing a lot of losses.

So we set out to design the next version...

The working principle of the device

The core working principle of the device is to switch between 4 states, i.e. every time the yellow button is pressed, the device will switch to the next state：(You can refer to the flow chart on the left)

A：Stop 1 → B：Rising → C：Stop 2 → D：Falling

Here are some special designs:

The red button

The red button is the emergency stop button, when it is pressed, the device will stop immediately regardless of its state and all LEDs will flash rapidly to warn the operator.

Collision Module

The collision module will be triggered when the device is lifted to its highest point. After a brief flash of the red LED, the device will switch to the Stop 2 state, meaning that if the yellow button is pressed after this point, the device will fall.

Laser + Photoresistor

This combination will be triggered when the device is lifted to its lowest point. After a brief flash of the red LED, the device will switch to the Stop 1 state, meaning that if the yellow button is pressed after this point, the device will rise.
